About Me

Hi! I am currently a research intern at UCSD Hao AI Lab, advised by Prof.Hao Zhang. I received my master degree in Robotics at UMich. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. of Robotics with honors from Cho Kochen Honors College at Zhejiang University in 2023.
